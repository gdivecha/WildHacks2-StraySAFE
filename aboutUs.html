About Us

Who are we?

StraySafe is a security system inplace to infrom missing and locate domestic Animals. For us at StraySafe our top priority is ensuring the safety of your pet. As well as creating a safe/controlled environment for pet owners to be able to report and find their missing animal ASAP. With our easy-to-use interface and accurate system for animal tracking the stress and worry of a missing animal will never come accross your mind.

FAQs

Q. How old is Safe Stray?
A. Safe Stray has been recovering lost animals since 2019, but officially launched in 2022

Q. Is Safe Stray an NGO (non-profit org)
A. No, Safe Stray does not charge users!

Q. Where does Safe Stray operate?
A. Currently, Safe Stray operates in Canada

What we look for in the Future?

Q. What do we plan to upgrade?
In the future we plan to implement a feature that can pinpoint a geological location of a pet via "Animal Tracker".

Q. Will this remain for Pet Users only?
A. No, We wish to expand our audience, so that non-users may be able to assist in locating pets.

Q. Can we make profiles for our pets?
A. Currently no, but we plan on adding it for pets in order to make the reporting easier.
